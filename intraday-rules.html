VWAP Direction20 ptsMANDATORY
VWAP is the institutional fair-value anchor. Long setups below VWAP and short setups above VWAP are rejected — this gate cannot be disabled.
VWAP = Σ(price × volume) ÷ Σ(volume) (session cumulative) Bull: price > VWAP · Bear: price < VWAP
